Office Automation System

Overview

Our school provided us a chance to get an internship in ChinaSoft International at the beginning of our senior year. Our main work in ChinaSoft was to build an OA system including email, contacts, meeting management, schedule, etc. I was the designer of our team, because I had gotten a website design course in my junior year and am interested in UI / UE.

At the end of our intern, our team got the Best Team Award because our UI was the best and we implemented all of the features.

My Role

In our team, I was the only UI designer and front-end programmer. Reading some relevant books, e.g. Don't Make Me Think, CSS Cookbook etc., was very helpful to me during this project. These books let me know how to design a user-friendly website, how to using CSS and HTML to implement my ideas and how to think like a designer.

I think this project is significant to me because it gave me a chance to take part in the whole process of design and implement a project. Furthermore, it let me know that I have some potential to be a UI / UE designer.
