Logo Design

When I began to redesign the logo of my website, I started with drawing some cartoon figures on my notebook. After a few times of tries, I started to draw a figure of fox, which is one of my favorite animals, and suddenly I realized that the shape of the fox I was drawing was in a shape of 'M', which is the initiate of my first name.So I took a little more time to carefully restructure the fox perfectly representing the letter of M and with a beautiful tail, which I also took time to carefully design.
