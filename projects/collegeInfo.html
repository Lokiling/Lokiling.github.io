UI of College Information

Overview

Often, college students cannot get messages and events' information of school in time. For instance, many of my classmates missed a lot of valuable lectures, because of the delayed inform. So my friend, Siyuan Jia, and I had an idea to solve this problem: building a website which will collect these kinds of information for college students. We thought this website should include some helpful features, such as lecture reminder, courses reminder, etc. So I started to design the UI to make the idea come true.

However, he was so lucky that he got a chance to study abroad in France. Due to the time differences and the difficulty to communicate, we could not accomplish it any more. But I still made a demo without back-end program.
