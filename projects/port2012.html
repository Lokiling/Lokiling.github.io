Portfolio 2012

Overview

This is my portfolio which I built last year. You can look it at here!

It took me about a week to think and design that website. I had tried some different schemes with different colors and various styles, and decided to use that style finally. I prefer the simple design without numerous unnecessary decorative elements. I got inspirations from the design of Google Now, which every information comes out within a white card. I think it is fabulous, so that I use a lot of "cards" in my portfolio as you see.

Designing things is always fun to me. I love doing it! It will give me a sense of achievement when I constructed it representing my ideas and show them to my friends! My dream is to be an interaction designer and make people's lives much easier.
