About Me

I achieved my bachelor degree in Software Engineering at Beijing University of Technology in 2011. I got an intern, as a designer of a project, in ChinaSoft International at the beginning of my senior year. After that, I found my dream is to be an interaction designer. I was lucky enough to spend one and a half years at Happy Elements, a famous social game company in China, after I graduated. As a product manager, I participated in development of two games. Most of my jobs in Happy Elements were data analysis, sketching, designing user interface and interaction.

I'm a Google Fan and I think most of Google's products are remarkable. The designs of their products are simpler and easier to use after their latest update. Moreover, Google's products actually do ease people's lives.

Now, my passion about human-centered design drives me towards pursuing a master degree in Human-Computer Interaction in U.S. I expect I can create fantastic things to enrich and facilitate people's lives in the future.
