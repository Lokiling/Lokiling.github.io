Overview

Hey! Welcome to my portfolio! The reason I build this website is to show my experience of UI and UX design and, more importantly, to pursue a Master's Degree in Human-Computer Interaction.

It took me about 1 week to think and design this website. I had try some different schemes, such as different color and diverse styles, and decided to use this style. I prefer the simple design without numerous inessential decorative elements. I got inspirations from the design of Google Now, which every information comes out within a white card. I think it is so lovely that I use a lot of "cards" in my portfolio as you see.

Designing things is always fun to me. I love design! It will give me a sense of achievement when I implement my ideas and show it to my friends! My dream is to be a UX designer and make people's lives much easier.
