Keywords

Interaction design, UI design, wireframe, front-end programming, user experience

Overview

Often, college students cannot get messages and events' information of school in time. For instance, many of my classmates missed a lot of valuable lecture, because of the delayed inform. So my friend, Siyuan Jia, and I had an idea to solve this problem: building a web site which will collect these kinds of information for college students. We thought this website should include some helpful feature, such as lecture reminder, courses reminder, etc. So I started to design the UI to make the idea came true.

However, he was so luck that he got a chance to study abroad in France. Due to the time differences and the difficulty to communicate, we could not complish it any more. But I still made a demo without back-end program.
