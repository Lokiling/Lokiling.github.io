About me

I am a creative, detail-oriented UX designer with a background in software engineering. I graduated from Indiana University with a master's degree in Human-Computer Interaction / Design.

Passionate about design and technology, I moved to the Bay area and currently working at Guidespark as a Product Designer. I believe design has the power to change people's lives and I want to leverage this power to make the world a better place.

Outside of design, I see myself as an explorer and always stay curious about the world. I enjoy traveling to experience different cultures and meet new friends from all over the world. You can also find me playing video and board games in my spare time because there are more fantasy worlds worth exploring.
